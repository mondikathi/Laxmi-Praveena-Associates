Overview

Why Start a Child Plan Early

Education costs in India have been rising steadily, and a dedicated child plan helps you save in a disciplined, goal-linked manner rather than relying on ad-hoc savings.

Most child education plans include a built-in protection feature: if the parent (policyholder) is no longer around, future premiums may be waived while the plan continues to pay out at the intended milestones — so your child's education is never compromised.

We help you map out anticipated milestones — school, undergraduate studies, postgraduate studies — and match them against plan payout schedules and premium affordability.

Who Should Consider This Plan?

Here's who typically benefits most from this type of cover:

  • Parents of young children planning ahead for higher education
  • Families wanting a protected, goal-based savings option
  • Parents who want education savings to continue even in their absence
  • Grandparents wishing to gift a head start to a grandchild's future
