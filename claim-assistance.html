Why Claim Support Matters

You're Not Alone When It Matters Most

A claim usually arises during a stressful or emotional time — a hospitalisation, a health emergency, or the loss of a loved one. Confusing paperwork shouldn't add to that stress.

As your insurance advisor, we don't disappear after the policy is issued. We remain your point of contact to help you or your nominee navigate the claim process — from the first phone call to final settlement follow-up.

  • Guidance on which documents are required for your specific claim type
  • Help filling and reviewing claim forms before submission
  • Coordination with the Tata AIA claims team on your behalf
  • Regular status updates until the claim is resolved
How Claim Assistance Works

4 Simple Steps to a Settled Claim

1

Inform Us Immediately

Call or WhatsApp us as soon as a claim event occurs — we'll guide you on the immediate next steps.

2

Documentation Support

We help you gather the required documents: policy details, medical/death certificates, ID proofs and claim forms.

3

Claim Submission

We assist in submitting the claim through the correct Tata AIA channel and keep a record of submission.

4

Follow-Up Until Resolved

We track the claim status and follow up on your behalf until it is settled.
