Overview

Saving With a Safety Net

Savings and investment-linked insurance plans combine two benefits in one: a life cover for your family, and a savings or investment component that helps you build a corpus over time.

These plans work well for medium to long-term goals — a home down payment, a wedding fund, a business goal, or simply building long-term wealth in a disciplined, structured way.

We help you understand the difference between guaranteed-return and market-linked options, so you can choose based on your comfort with risk and your specific financial goals.
