Who We Are

A Local Advisor You Can Actually Reach

Laxmi Praveena Associates is an authorised advisory service for Tata AIA Life Insurance, based in Kukatpally and serving families across Hyderabad. We help individuals and families choose the right mix of life cover, health protection, child education plans, retirement solutions and savings products — based on their goals, income and life stage.

Rather than pushing a one-size-fits-all product, we take time to understand your situation: your dependents, your existing cover, your monthly budget and your long-term goals. Only then do we recommend a plan, and we explain every clause in plain Telugu or English, whichever you're comfortable with.
