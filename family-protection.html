Overview

Protecting Every Member of Your Household

Family protection isn't a single product — it's a combination of the right life cover, health insurance and savings tailored to your household's structure: spouse, children, and sometimes dependent parents.

We assess your family's complete financial picture — existing policies, monthly expenses, children's ages and long-term goals — before recommending a combination of plans that closes any protection gaps.

The goal is simple: if something unexpected happens to the primary earner, the family should be able to maintain their home, continue children's education, and meet ongoing expenses without financial stress.

Who Should Consider This Plan?

Here's who typically benefits most from this type of cover:

  • Families with young children and a home loan
  • Households where one person is the primary earner
  • Families wanting to consolidate multiple insurance needs with one advisor
  • Anyone who hasn't reviewed their cover in the last 2-3 years
